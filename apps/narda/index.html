Narda

Two snaps merge into one frame

Unexpected juxtapositions, playful storytelling.

Narda lets you take two snaps that merge into a frame. Moments collide, perspectives shift, and little surprises emerge. It's about joyful discovery.

Free • iOS

Features

Half-frame shooting

Two snaps become one 2:3 photo — a playful split-frame composition.

Multi-exposure

Layer exposures for unexpected blends and dreamlike images.

Manual film advance

Wind forward between frames to keep a tactile shooting rhythm.
