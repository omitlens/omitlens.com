Purple - Privacy

Effective Date: 2025-01-20

Purple ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS app and make in-app purchase subscriptions.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. Our app is designed to function without requiring any user data, ensuring your privacy and security.

In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchase subscriptions through Apple’s App Store. All payment processing and subscription management are handled by Apple Inc. (“Apple”) under its own terms and privacy policies. We do not receive or store any billing information or payment details.

For more information on how Apple processes your payment information, please review Apple’s Privacy Policy at: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Third-Party Services

While we do not collect any data, third-party services such as Apple’s App Store may collect and process your information. These services are governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no risk of unauthorized access or data breaches related to personal information from our app.

Your Privacy Rights

As our app does not collect any user information, there are no data rights or requests applicable under privacy laws like GDPR, CCPA, or similar regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated policy will be effective as of the "Effective Date" listed above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Thank you for using Purple.
