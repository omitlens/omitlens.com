A film ritual in your pocket

Recreating the imperfect beauty of analog photography, one uncertain shutter press at a time.

Horika is not just a filter app. It rebuilds the tactile shooting experience of a film camera: the feel of advancing film with your finger, the moment the shutter fires, and the small uncertainty that comes with analog ritual.

Every shot may bring light leaks, grain, color shifts, or blur. These are not bugs—they are character.

Core experiences

🎞

Thumb advance & randomness

Advance the film with your thumb — a tiny ritual that recreates the tactile feel of physically winding the roll. Every shutter press is a small random experiment.

✨

Toy camera texture

Layered light leaks, vignetting, grain, scratches, color shifts, and film borders. Authentic toy camera character in every shot.

🎨

16+ films

Normal, Pro, Rare, and Seasonal films. Each with distinct visual character: from dreamy purples to warm golds to cool teals.

📐

Multiple frame formats

Simulate common 120 format frame sizes: 6×6 square, 6×4.5 panoramic, and wide format. Each feels different.

🔧

Film Lab

Create, import, and manage your own custom films. Design your own film presets with total creative control.

📷

Holka mode

Built-in Holka mode for a capture experience closer to a real toy camera. Also features multi-exposure and lens filter simulations.
